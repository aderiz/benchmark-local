 Tower Building of the Little Rock Arsenal is a building in Little Rock , Arkansas , United States . It was built in 1840 as part of the Little Rock Arsenal , a military installation established to defend the western frontier . The building is one of the oldest surviving structures in the city and was listed on the National Register of Historic Places in 1970 .
 The tower building served as the arsenal 's main administrative center and was used to store weapons and ammunition . During the Civil War , the arsenal changed hands between Union and Confederate forces several times . After the war , the building was repurposed for various civic uses . It has since been restored and is now part of the MacArthur Park Historic District .
 The building features a distinctive tower structure that rises above the main block , giving it its name . The architecture reflects the military building practices of the mid @-@ 19th century , with thick walls and functional design elements . The interior has been modified several times over the years but retains much of its original character .
 Artificial intelligence research has been the subject of much debate since the field 's inception in the 1950s . Early AI researchers were optimistic about achieving human @-@ level intelligence within a few decades , but progress proved to be much slower than anticipated . The field experienced several periods of reduced funding and interest , known as " AI winters " , when expectations outpaced results .
 Modern AI has shifted focus from general intelligence to narrow applications that can perform specific tasks at or above human level . Machine learning , particularly deep learning with neural networks , has driven most recent advances . Large language models trained on vast amounts of text data have shown remarkable abilities in natural language understanding and generation .
 The development of transformer architectures in 2017 marked a turning point in natural language processing . These models use self @-@ attention mechanisms to process sequences of tokens in parallel , enabling much larger models to be trained efficiently . The scaling of these models has led to emergent capabilities that were not explicitly trained for .
 Renewable energy technologies have advanced significantly in the past two decades . Solar photovoltaic costs have fallen by more than 90 percent since 2010 , making solar power competitive with fossil fuels in many regions . Wind energy has also seen dramatic cost reductions , with offshore wind emerging as a major growth area .
 Energy storage remains a key challenge for renewable energy deployment . Battery technologies , particularly lithium @-@ ion batteries , have improved in both capacity and cost , enabling the growth of electric vehicles and grid @-@ scale storage . Research into alternative storage methods , including pumped hydro , compressed air , and hydrogen , continues to progress .
 The transition to renewable energy has significant implications for global economics and geopolitics . Countries with abundant renewable resources may gain economic advantages , while those dependent on fossil fuel exports face economic restructuring . The renewable energy transition also requires massive investments in grid infrastructure and workforce development .
 Climate science has established that human activities , primarily the burning of fossil fuels , are the dominant cause of global warming observed since the mid @-@ 20th century . Global surface temperatures have increased by approximately 1 @.@ 1 degrees Celsius above pre @-@ industrial levels . The effects include rising sea levels , more frequent extreme weather events , and shifts in ecosystems and biodiversity .
 Quantum computing represents a fundamentally different approach to computation . Unlike classical computers that use bits representing 0 or 1 , quantum computers use quantum bits ( qubits ) that can exist in superposition , representing both states simultaneously . This property , combined with quantum entanglement , allows quantum computers to solve certain problems exponentially faster than classical computers .
 Current quantum computers are still in the noisy intermediate @-@ scale quantum ( NISQ ) era , with limited numbers of qubits and high error rates . Error correction techniques are being developed but require significant overhead in additional qubits . Despite these limitations , quantum computers have demonstrated quantum advantage for specific computational tasks .
 The potential applications of quantum computing span many fields , including cryptography , drug discovery , materials science , and optimization problems . Quantum algorithms such as Shor 's algorithm could break widely used encryption schemes , prompting research into post @-@ quantum cryptography . In chemistry , quantum simulations could accelerate the discovery of new materials and drugs .
 Space exploration has entered a new era with the rise of commercial spaceflight companies . Reusable rocket technology has dramatically reduced the cost of reaching orbit , opening up new possibilities for scientific research , satellite deployment , and eventually human settlement beyond Earth . Multiple nations and private companies are developing plans for lunar bases and Mars missions .
 The James Webb Space Telescope , launched in 2021 , has provided unprecedented views of the early universe , exoplanet atmospheres , and stellar formation . Its infrared capabilities allow it to see through dust clouds and observe objects that formed shortly after the Big Bang . Early results have already challenged some existing models of galaxy formation .
